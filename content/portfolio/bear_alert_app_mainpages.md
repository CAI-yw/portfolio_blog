The main purpose of this project is to **allow users to share wildlife sightings/observations** and to **help hikers and tourists prepare for potential encounters with wild animals**. The app provides real-time updates, a user-friendly interface, and a secure platform for submitting observations.

---

## Main Pages of the Application

### 1. User Login

This page allows registered users to sign in to their account.  
Users can access personalized features, such as submitting new observations or viewing their saved sightings.

[![Sign In Page](https://i.postimg.cc/MHGWpZDY/Screenshot-2025-10-28-at-9-49-05-PM.png)](https://postimg.cc/KkdX92CK)

---

### 2. User Signup

New users can register for an account via this page.  
The signup process includes email verification to ensure account security, allowing users to safely participate in reporting animal sightings.

[![Registration Page](https://i.postimg.cc/y6J1txDz/Screenshot-2025-10-28-at-9-49-31-PM.png)](https://postimg.cc/Hjg17p0S)

---

### 3. Map View

The map displays **animal sightings** submitted by users.  
Hikers and tourists can quickly see where wildlife activity has been reported, helping them plan safe routes and avoid potentially dangerous areas.

[![Map Showing Animal Observations (with fake data)](https://i.postimg.cc/7Ls4Qr4Q/Screenshot-2025-10-28-at-10-00-05-PM.png)](https://postimg.cc/bGt4sMsQ)

---

### 4. Adding a New Observation

Users can submit a new wildlife sighting through this dashboard.  
The form allows entering details such as **animal type, location, date, and any details**, which are then displayed on the map.

[![Dashboard for Uploading a New Observation](https://i.postimg.cc/t49Hq44Y/Screenshot-2025-10-28-at-10-00-32-PM.png)](https://postimg.cc/GHgfzrYC)
